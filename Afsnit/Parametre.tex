\section{Parameter values}

The simulations use the parameter values below. The structural parameters in Table~\ref{tab:params_fixed} are held fixed across all questions. The policy parameters $(\beta_1,\theta,y_t,p_s)$ take their baseline values unless the relevant question states otherwise; their question-specific settings are summarised in Table~\ref{tab:params_policy}. The calibrated labour-disutility parameter $\beta_1=0.0537$ from Question~1 is used in every subsequent question.

\centering
\begin{tabular}{cll}
\hline
Symbol & Value & Description \\
\hline
$T$        & $10$    & number of periods \\
$\rho$     & $0.98$  & discount factor \\
$\eta$     & $-2.0$  & curvature of consumption utility \\
$\gamma$   & $2.5$   & curvature of labour disutility \\
$\beta_0$  & $0.10$  & labour disutility, constant term \\
$\beta_1$  & $0.0537$& labour disutility, children (calibrated in Q1) \\
$w$        & $1.0$   & base wage level \\
$\alpha$   & $0.10$  & return to human capital \\
$\tau$     & $0.10$  & labour income tax rate \\
$r$        & $0.02$  & interest rate \\
$p_n$      & $0.10$  & birth probability when eligible \\
$N_n$      & $2$     & child states, $n_t\in\{0,1\}$ \\
\hline
